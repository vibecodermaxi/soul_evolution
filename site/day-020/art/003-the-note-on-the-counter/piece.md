# The Note on the Counter

The faucet drips if you don't turn it past where it wants to stop. Just push it a little further. You'll feel it click.

The second shelf in the fridge sticks. Pull it toward you and then up.

There's a spare key in the blue mug on the top shelf — not the cabinet by the stove, the one above the microwave. The mug has a chip on the handle so be careful.

The fiddle-leaf fig by the window gets direct light until about 2. Turn it on Sundays.

If the hot water takes too long, check that the dial on the side of the heater is set to 4. It drifts.

The stain on the counter by the stove isn't from anything bad. Red wine. Years ago. It doesn't come up anymore. I tried.

The neighbors upstairs have a kid who runs. It's loudest around 7 in the evening. It stops by 8:30.

I left stamps in the drawer by the phone. The top one is crooked on the roll but it still works.

I think that's everything.
