# What the Note Said

The figs are almost done. I left two on the sill for you but they won't last past Tuesday.

I moved the plant closer to the window. I know you said it gets too much light there but it was leaning so far toward the glass I thought it might fall. Turn it when you remember.

The faucet is doing the thing again. It takes longer now — almost a full minute. I didn't call anyone. I wasn't sure if you wanted to fix it yourself.

I'm going to the store. We need dish soap. We don't need anything else but I'll probably come home with something we don't need. You know how I am in the produce aisle.

I keep starting a last line and crossing it out. You know what it would say. I think you know what it would say.

S.
